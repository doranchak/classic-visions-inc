Welcome to the Classic Visions Native American Calendar of Historical Events

(soon to be available as a downloadable program for your computer or on floppy disk or CDROM)

Coming in August 1997, Classic Visions will be offering a Native-American Historical and Notable Events Calendar for sale as a downloadable program or on floppy disk. Featuring the artwork of Native-American artist Bob Wakanabo, Ojibwa, this calendar includes the months of August through December 1997 and the entire year of 1998, chronicling significant and notable events in the history of Native-Americans, events which detail the struggle, the triumphs and defeats of some of the most noble and heroic people in the history of the world. Follow and commemorate with this calendar, the lives of Tecumseh, Joseph Brant, Pontiac, the Navajo Long Walk, the Cherokee Trail of Tears, the triumphs and achievements of the great leaders among Native-Americans. You will come to learn of the heroism of Native-American Crispus Attucks and the code talkers of World Wars I and II.

Each month of the calendar contains original artwork by Bob Wakanabo, whose works have sold worldwide, and who created this calendar series of pictures specifically for the Native-American Historical and Notable Events Calendar.

This program contains a special screen saver option which allows you to use any of the pictures which you prefer as a screen saver. Or you can change them monthly, done automatically by the calendar program.
