The Great American Indian Bible (available in print copies for you to own and treasure for generations)

The Indian Heritage Council, has produced a collection of many of the sayings of spiritual and political leaders in the recorded history of Native-Americans of all tribes and nations. In twenty eight chapters entitled The Great American Indian Bible, this collection not only contains these timeless words, but it also includes devotions, such as the Kwaital-Bannock-Shoshoni Indian Prayer, the Sayings of Sage Elk, Cherokee Medicine Man, advice from Indian elders of today, and other depictions of Native-American lives, as seen through their eyes. The Great American Indian Bible, is offered through Classic Visions, Inc., the only Internet distributor of this timeless collection. Believe it or not, the Great American Indian Bible is not currently available at stores.
