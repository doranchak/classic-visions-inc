New offerings here at Classic Visions this month are limited signed edition prints of artwork by Native American artist, Bob Wakanabo.

Wakanabo's works are classic examples of the best of Native American art. Seen here is one of the many prints which Mr. Wakanabo has created in the past few years.

Mr. Wakanabo, an Ojibwa Native American artist living in Cass Lakes Minnesota on the Leech Lake Reservation, has been painting for several years, and was awarded the 1994 Artist of the Year for the Great Lakes Region Native American Fish and Wildlife Society.

His works are shown in museums across the USA and have been sold around the world.

All of Mr. Wakanabo's themes have historical backgrounds and each picture he creates comes with a story explaining his work of art. You may order copies of these extraordinary prints right here on Classic Vision's web page.
